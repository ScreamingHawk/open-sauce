Here's the Gregg's Instant Dessert Vanilla packet.

![Gregg's Instant Dessert Vanilla packet]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/images/greggs.webp)

If you can't find the Gregg's Instant Dessert Vanilla packet, you can make it from scratch with the following:

- 2 cups cream
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch (cornflour)
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla essence

1. Mix the cornstarch, sugar, and salt together.
1. Pour the cream into a saucepan. Heat over medium heat until you see tiny bubbles starting to form around the edges (don't let it boil vigorously).
1. Slowly whisk the dry mixture into the warm cream. Keep whisking constantly!
1. Continue to cook over medium-low heat until the mixture thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon (about 3â€“5 minutes).
1. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla.
1. Pour into bowls. Chill in the fridge for at least an hour.
